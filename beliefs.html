What We Believe

The principles that guide our mission of raising Kingdom citizens.

Our Fundamental Beliefs

1. The Kingdom of God Is God's Government

We believe the Kingdom of God is God's government, His system of administering His will on earth, and Jesus Christ is its King.

Scripture: Matthew 6:33, Mark 1:14-15

2. Every Person Has Divine Purpose and Capacity

We believe every human being was created with dignity, purpose, and the capacity to influence the world through their God-given gifts.

Scripture: Genesis 1:26-28, Jeremiah 29:11

3. Righteousness Is The Foundation of Lasting Prosperity

We believe righteousness is the foundation for true prosperity, social stability, and national transformation.

Scripture: Proverbs 14:34, Matthew 6:33

4. Divine Wisdom Produces Extraordinary Results

We believe wisdom is greater than information and that divine wisdom empowers people to create solutions beyond human limitations.

Scripture: Proverbs 4:7, James 1:5

5. God's Kingdom Provides Answers To Humanity's Problems

We believe poverty, corruption, injustice, ignorance, and hopelessness are challenges that can be overcome through Kingdom principles and power.

Scripture: Acts 4:34-35, Isaiah 61:1

6. Kingdom Citizens Are Called To Become Solutions

We believe God's people are called to express His wisdom, creativity, and power by bringing solutions to the world's challenges.

Scripture: Matthew 5:14-16, Romans 8:19

The 10-Day Kingdom Leadership Training

The Kingdom City Academy 10-Day Training is designed to transform the way participants understand themselves, God’s Kingdom, purpose, leadership, and economic responsibility.


Through intensive teaching, practical application, and guided mentorship, participants are equipped to move from limitation into purpose, productivity, and influence.

What You Will Experience

Renew Your Mind

Discover Kingdom perspectives that replace limiting beliefs with wisdom, purpose, and possibility.

Romans 12:2

Discover Your Purpose

Understand your identity, gifts, and assignment as a citizen of God's Kingdom.

Develop Practical Skills

Gain principles for leadership, productivity, financial responsibility, and community transformation.

Become A Solution Provider

Learn how to apply wisdom and creativity to address real problems around you.

Who Should Attend?

  • Individuals seeking personal transformation and purpose.
  • Leaders who desire to influence society positively.
  • Entrepreneurs and professionals seeking Kingdom principles for success.
  • Young people preparing to create meaningful impact.
  • Anyone committed to becoming part of a solution-driven community.

Kingdom Development Endowment

A portion of the training investment contributes to the Kingdom Development Endowment, a long-term empowerment system designed to create economic opportunities, support community development initiatives, and build sustainable solutions.


The vision is to develop a Kingdom-based economic platform where people collectively contribute resources toward projects that improve lives and strengthen communities.

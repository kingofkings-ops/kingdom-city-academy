About Kingdom City Academy

Kingdom City Academy exists to raise righteous, competent and influential citizens who reflect the government of God in every sphere of society. We believe the Kingdom of God is not merely a religion but God's system for transforming lives, communities and nations through righteousness, wisdom and purpose.

What We Believe

Kingdom

Righteousness is the foundation of lasting prosperity.

Purpose

Leadership

Kingdom citizens are servant leaders.

Transformation

We are called to become solutions to humanity's problems.

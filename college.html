About The College

The College of Strategic Theological Studies is the educational and research arm of Kingdom City Academy, designed to promote continuous learning, deeper understanding of God's Kingdom, and the development of leaders who can apply wisdom to real-world challenges.


The College combines biblical principles, strategic thinking, leadership development, and practical application to prepare individuals for meaningful influence in society.

Our Focus Areas

Kingdom Studies

Exploring the Kingdom of God, its principles, values, and implications for life and society.

Leadership Development

Preparing leaders who serve with wisdom, integrity, and vision.

Research & Innovation

Encouraging research that produces practical solutions to societal challenges.

Strategic Application

Applying knowledge and wisdom to transform communities, institutions, and nations.

A Culture Of Learning

Kingdom City Academy believes that information alone is not enough. True transformation requires wisdom—the ability to apply knowledge correctly to produce meaningful results.


Through continuous education, research, mentorship, and practical engagement, learners are equipped to become effective contributors in their fields of influence.

Future Development

As the Academy grows, the College of Strategic Theological Studies will expand into structured programs, research publications, digital courses, and leadership certifications.
